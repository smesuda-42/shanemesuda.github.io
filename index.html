About Me

I’m a Game Designer with over a decade of experience in mobile and live service games, blending design thinking with a deep QA background. I specialize in systems design, feature design, and progression tuning, with a strong focus on clarity, player motivation, and long-term engagement.

My strength is turning complex mechanics into intuitive player experiences and collaborating closely with engineering, art, and product teams to ship features that perform well in live environments.

Selected Projects

Legendary: Game of Heroes

Role: Game Designer

Led the design of multiple core systems and features including Hero Skills, Mythic Legends, Mythic Legend Relics, and a new series of Cudgels as long-term chase items.

Problem: Declining player sentiment and shrinking active player population.

  • Designed Hero Skills to deepen combat strategy and build diversity
  • Created Mythic Legends and Relics to provide long-term progression goals
  • Designed a new series of Cudgels as a long-term chase item for veteran players
  • Authored design specs and collaborated with engineering and live ops

Results: The new Cudgels series improved player retention and satisfaction. Mythic Legend Relics introduced a new chase item for veteran players and revitalized endgame engagement.

Legendary: Heroes Unchained

Role: Game Designer

Designed the foundational rewards economy for a new game, establishing how players earned and spent resources across progression and events.

Problem: The game launched without a defined rewards economy, requiring a full system to support progression, engagement, and monetization.

  • Defined reward types, sources, and sinks
  • Built early tuning models and progression curves
  • Worked cross-functionally with product and engineering to implement systems

Results & Learnings: While overall game performance was limited, this work established a complete economy framework and provided key learnings around reward pacing, inflation control, and player motivation in early-stage systems.
